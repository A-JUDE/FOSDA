About FOSDA

The Organization name is; FOUNDATION FOR SOCIO-CULTURAL DEVELOPMENT AND ALTERNATIVE ECONOMIC EMPOWERMENT. It was formed and registered in 2014 in Nigeria. The HEAD/CEO of the organization is PATIENCE MAINA CALEB.

Foundation for sociocultural Development and alternative Economic Empowerment is non-profit organization that was registered to empower youth and women in the area of Education making learning environments esay for sudents who have interest in learning but don't have access to reading materials. We establish both E and Manual Libraries to encourage them support their zeal and making learning easy for them and for those in correctional centers being there should not be a hindrance to achieve your goals especially in the areas of education Nigeria should be society where materials for learning should become a thing of the past as it said readers are leaders study to show yourself approved a work man that does not to be ashamed righly dividing the words of truth you know the truth through studies and research knowledge is power and we are here to support people who have interests in knowledge by supplying necessary materials.

AREA OF FOCUS

We focus on Women and Youths for the purpose of empowering them to be self-reliant by educating them and also enhancing their skills in areas of agriculture, carpentry, tailoring and various other entrepreneurship skills. The organization also provides counseling services for troubled people and creates opportunities for interaction and socialization. In trying to achieve these, the organization promotes cooperation with other Non-Governmental Organizations, National and International Agencies, and Voluntary Agencies with similar objectives and partners with them in bringing about socio-cultural and economic development to needing communities in Nigeria.
